Floating Shiny Knot

Millions of environment map textures with Google Street View panoramas

Click and drag to move the camera, scroll to zoom in and out.
Press R to randomize, Space to toggle animation.
Use search box to find addresses, or click on the map to select a new location.
Each location has a unique URL which you can share.
